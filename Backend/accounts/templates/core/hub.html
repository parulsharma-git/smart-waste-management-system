Make your city cleaner, greener & smarter 🌱

🌍 Importance of Recycling

Recycling helps reduce pollution, conserve natural resources, and save energy. It also decreases landfill waste and protects our environment for future generations.

💡 Tip: Always separate waste at home before disposal

📊 Did You Know?

  • 🌳 Recycling 1 ton paper saves 17 trees
  • ⚡ Plastic recycling saves up to 80% energy
  • 🌱 Composting reduces waste by 30%

🗂 Waste Segregation

  • 🟤 Dry Waste: Plastic, paper, glass
  • 🟢 Wet Waste: Food & organic waste
  • ⚠ Hazardous: Batteries, chemicals
  • 💻 E-Waste: Electronics & gadgets

♻ Plastic Recycling

  • Clean plastic before disposal
  • Avoid single-use plastic
  • Use reusable items
  • Check recycle symbols

🌱 Composting

Convert kitchen waste into natural fertilizer. It improves soil and reduces landfill waste.

🚮 Smart Practices

  • Report garbage via TrashBin
  • Join cleanliness drives
  • Educate others
  • Support eco initiatives

🌟 Our Mission

Promoting awareness, responsible waste disposal, and community participation for a cleaner environment.
